Surah 94:5
Added: 2025-10-22
فَإِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا
Fa‑inna maʿa al‑ʿusri yusran
So indeed, with hardship comes ease. — Sahih International
اللہ نے ہمارے لئے
This short tafsir snippet reassures that every difficulty is accompanied by relief and highlights the promise of gradual easing after trials.
